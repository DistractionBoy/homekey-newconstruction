# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
A landing page for Homekey Inspections promoting their new construction inspection bundle offering.

## Product Overview
A high-converting marketing landing page at `homekeyinspections.com/new-construction` designed to
educate and convert new-build home buyers in Northern Virginia into booked clients for Homekey
Inspections' three-stage "New Construction Bundle."

## Target Users
- **Primary:** New-build home buyers in Northern Virginia who are purchasing a newly constructed home
  and want independent inspection coverage throughout the build lifecycle.
- **Secondary:** Spanish-speaking home buyers (bilingual EN/ES support required throughout).

## Core Value Proposition
Independent, ACI-certified inspections at every stage of a new home build — by the same inspector,
with the same thermal camera, and the same ISO-accredited lab — providing continuous protection from
foundation to final warranty claim.

## Product Goals
1. Educate buyers on the conflict of interest inherent in builder-preferred inspectors
2. Explain the three-stage bundle and why each stage matters
3. Surface transparent, interactive bundle pricing to reduce booking friction
4. Drive conversions via a "Reserve your bundle" CTA
5. Build trust through certifications, bilingual support, and real testimonials
6. Rank locally for NoVA new-build communities (SEO grid)

### The Problem
Side-by-side comparison card:
- **Builder's inspector:** Paid by builder, speed-optimized for closing, findings rarely delay deals,
  no thermal imaging, report goes to builder first.
- **HomeKey (independent):** Paid by buyer, quality-optimized for 30-year ownership, findings create
  leverage before closing, thermal + moisture meters standard, report goes to buyer first.

### The Bundle — Three-Stage Journey
1. **Pre-drywall** (Week ~8–12 of construction): Framing, plumbing, electrical, HVAC rough-in defects
   caught before drywall seals them in.
2. **Pre-settlement** (Days before closing): Full inspection with thermal imaging + ISO/IEC 17025
   lab mold testing; findings become the buyer's punch list.
3. **11-month warranty** (Before builder warranty expires): Catch settling defects and systems
   failures while the builder is still obligated to fix them.

### Bundle Pricing Calculator
- Inputs: Square footage slider, build community selector, optional add-ons (radon, sewer scope)
- Outputs: Itemized line items + 15% bundle discount applied automatically
- Default example: Pre-drywall $350 + Pre-settlement $525 + 11-month $425 = $1,300 → **$1,105**
  (saving $195)
- CTA: "Reserve this bundle →"
